About Us

We help you meet your timelines

Whether on a month-to-month or as needed basis, we can help you meet the UGA jurisdiction to issue permits within 90 days of application. With increasing pressure on jurisdictions to meet state-mandated permitting timelines, timely plan review is more important than ever. Our service provides a scalable solution that helps jurisdictions increase review capacity, reduce backlogs, and keep permit applications moving toward approval.

Services

Our Solution

We provide remote third-party plan review services designed to help local jurisdictions maintain predictable permitting timelines and address plan review backlogs. Services are available through flexible month-to-month agreements or targeted backlog-clearing engagements, depending on the jurisdiction's needs. Our primary service commitment is a maximum 15-business-day plan review timeline, helping jurisdictions move applications through the review process efficiently and predictably.
